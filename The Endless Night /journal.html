► DAY 1 - 06/22/1993

I woke up at exactly 2:17 AM. Something felt wrong. The house was too quiet.

Mom and Dad weren't in their room. Tommy wasn't in his. No one is answering their phones.

I walked outside. The streetlights were on, but every house was dark. No one anywhere.

It's been hours. The sun should have risen by now.

I drove around town. The school, the mall, the police station - all empty.

I'm back home now. I don't know what to do.

WHY AM I THE ONLY ONE LEFT?

► DAY 2 - 06/23/1993

Still no sunrise. My watch says it's been 24 hours, but it feels longer.

I've been driving around with a flashlight. Found stores still stocked with food.

The power is on, but TV shows nothing but static. Radio too.

I've been yelling, honking car horns. Nothing.

I swear I saw something move at the edge of my flashlight beam.

Probably just a cat or something.

I'm going to sleep in the living room with all the lights on.

► DAY 4 - 06/25/1993

I didn't write yesterday. Couldn't bring myself to.

I'm not alone here.

I saw it standing in my neighbor's yard. Tall. Too tall.

It was just... watching me. I took a picture but it didn't come out right.

Just a black smudge where it was standing.

I'm staying inside now. I've boarded up the windows.

IT KNOWS MY NAME

► DAY 7 - 06/28/1993

The town is changing. Streets don't go where they should.

I tried to drive to the highway yesterday, but I kept ending up back at the train tracks.

The old train tracks. No matter which direction I drove.

There's fog now. Thick fog that seems to glow faintly.

I've seen more of the shadow people. They stand at a distance, but there are more now.

I found writing on my bathroom mirror this morning.

It said "CROSS THE TRACKS"

I didn't write it.

► DAY 9 - 06/30/1993

I saw something new last night. Blue lights.

They hover in the air, like fireflies but bigger and brighter.

They don't seem threatening like the shadows.

One floated right up to my window. When I put my hand near the glass, it pulsed brighter.

It's hard to explain, but it felt like it was trying to communicate.

The lights all seem to drift toward the train tracks before disappearing.

I think they want me to follow them.

I FOUND A [REDACTED] ON THE KITCHEN TABLE

► DAY 13 - 07/04/1993

Happy 4th of July. Not that it matters now.

The shadow figures have gotten closer. They're at the edge of my yard now.

I can sometimes hear them whispering, but can't make out words.

The blue lights came again. This time, they formed an arrow pointing toward the tracks.

I've been looking at the stars. They're wrong. Constellations I don't recognize.

I found an old map of Millfield in Dad's office. There's a facility marked beyond the tracks.

It's not on any modern maps. Dad never mentioned it.

I think I need to go there.

► DAY 14 - 07/05/1993

This will be my last entry for now.

The shadows are all around the house. I can see them through the gaps in the boards.

But the blue lights have formed a path. A safe passage through them.

I'm going to follow it to the train tracks.

Whatever is beyond them, it has to be better than slowly waiting to die here.

If anyone finds this journal, my name is Christopher Manly.

I was 17 years old when the world ended.

I'm going to the facility. I think it's called Project N
