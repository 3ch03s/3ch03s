INCIDENT REPORT #22 - SECURITY LOG

DATE: 07/18/2008 | LOCATION: SET C - "THE HALL OF MIRRORS"

REPORTING OFFICER: J. MONROE

INCIDENT SUMMARY:

At approximately 3:17 AM on July 18th, motion sensors in Set C triggered a security alert. As the on-duty security officer, I responded to investigate.

Upon arrival, I found all lights functioning normally, but all seven mirrors in the set appeared to be displaying different reflections than they should have given the room's contents. Specifically:

  • Mirror #1: Reflected the set normally except empty of personnel (including myself)
  • Mirror #2: Showed what appeared to be the same set but at a different time of day
  • Mirror #3: Displayed what appeared to be an urban landscape with unusual architecture. Structures appeared to be made of reflective materials and unusual angles
  • Mirror #4: Showed the set with approximately 12-15 silhouetted figures standing in a semi-circle
  • Mirrors #5-7: Too dark to clearly observe but appeared to contain movement

I immediately contacted Dr. Vex as per protocol. While waiting for his arrival, I observed that Mirror #4 showed the silhouetted figures appearing to watch me and moving positions slightly when I moved. I maintained a distance of at least 6 feet from all mirrors as per security guidelines.

Dr. Vex arrived at 3:42 AM accompanied by Elias Martin. Dr. Vex appeared excited rather than concerned by my report. He instructed Martin to "prepare the recording equipment" while he examined the mirrors.

Dr. Vex approached Mirror #3 and placed his hand on the surface. He claimed to feel "a slight vibration" and stated that "the boundary is thinning nicely." He then produced a small silver object (possibly a tuning fork) and struck it before holding it to the mirror. This produced a sound I can only describe as similar to distant voices all speaking simultaneously.

At this point, I observed the silhouettes in Mirror #4 all turn their attention to Dr. Vex. One figure separated from the group and approached the "front" of the mirror. The figure appeared humanoid but with proportions that seemed wrong - elongated limbs and possibly additional appendages that were difficult to discern in the low light.

Dr. Vex addressed the mirror directly, saying: "We are almost ready. The final performance is being prepared as agreed. The door will open on November 11th."

The figure in Mirror #4 appeared to nod before the reflection suddenly returned to normal. All mirrors simultaneously reverted to showing accurate reflections of the room.

Dr. Vex instructed me to classify this incident and to increase security on Set C. He seemed extremely pleased with the event, referring to it as "confirmation that we're on schedule."

ACTION TAKEN:

- Additional security camera installed facing Mirror #4

- Set C restricted to authorized personnel only

- Log entry classified at Dr. Vex's request

FOLLOW-UP:

I have requested reassignment to daytime security duty. I no longer feel comfortable with night shifts. The mirrors seem to show different reflections when I'm alone, and I've begun seeing silhouettes in reflective surfaces outside of Set C as well. Yesterday I saw a figure standing behind me in my rearview mirror while driving home, but when I turned around the car was empty.

ADDENDUM (07/25/2008): Officer Monroe has been reported missing. His vehicle was found abandoned near the studio with the engine running. The rearview mirror was missing from the vehicle.
