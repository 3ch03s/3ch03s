  2. Arrange seven candles at the marked positions. The seventh should be placed at the center altar.
  3. All participants must recite the Threshold Verse in unison while facing Mirror #3: "The stars align, the lightway bridges worlds, the Audience awaits our performance."

  9. To close the session, extinguish the candles in reverse order, ending with the center altar.
